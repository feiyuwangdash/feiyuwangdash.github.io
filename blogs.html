Do UEFA Referees Favor Players From Their Own Country? An Econometric Look at Five Seasons of Champions League and Europa League

2026-05-07

The 2026 Bayern–PSG semifinal raised an old fan suspicion: does a referee call fewer fouls on his countrymen? Using FBref data on 1,531 UCL/UEL matches from 2021–22 to 2025–26, the descriptive comparison shows a 20% gap in fouls per 90 between same-country and other player-matches — but a two-way fixed-effect specification (player + match FE) erases it entirely. A team-level win-rate effect appears, then collapses once a club-quality control is added. A worked example of how a 20% raw gap can dissolve once you account for whom you’re actually comparing.
